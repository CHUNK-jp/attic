Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for one job — finding your files — and doing it better than anything else.

Instant search

Results appear in under 50ms. Full-text across filenames, content, metadata, and tags — all at once.

100% private

Your files never leave your machine. No cloud, no analytics, no telemetry. We literally can't see your data.

Content-aware

Understands what's inside your files — not just their names. Searches PDFs, code, docs, spreadsheets, and more.

Native macOS

Built from the ground up for Mac. Integrates with Spotlight, respects system permissions, and feels native.

Global shortcut

Press ⌘K from anywhere on your Mac to summon Attic instantly. Customizable to any shortcut you prefer.

Always up to date

The index updates in real time as you add, edit, or remove files. No manual re-indexing, ever.
